Survey Equipment Used

Survey Chain

Used for linear measurements in traditional land surveys. Provides accurate ground distance measurement.

Cross Staff

Used to set out right angles in surveying. Helps in alignment and boundary marking.

Theodolite

Precision instrument for measuring horizontal and vertical angles. Used in triangulation surveys.

Total Station

Modern digital surveying instrument. Measures distance, angles and coordinates accurately.

GNSS Rover

Satellite-based surveying system. Provides high precision geo-location data.

CORS Network

Continuously Operating Reference Stations. Used for high accuracy GPS correction data.

Measuring Tape

Basic instrument for short distance measurements. Used in field verification and boundary marking.
